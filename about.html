About Me

Personalized physical therapy built around people, not rushed appointments.

The person behind Roll Ready PT

Roll Ready PT is designed for people who want focused, thoughtful care and a better understanding of their body.

Brian built the practice around one idea: care should meet you where you already are, not add another errand to your day. That's why treatment often happens right at your gym — before or after class, between rounds, or at open mat.

For more hands-on or private work, Brian also treats out of a pop-up van parked right in the gym lot.

Credentials

  • Doctor of Physical Therapy (DPT) — Creighton University, 2022
  • Certified Integrative Dry Needling Practitioner
  • 1st Degree BJJ Black Belt — Marcelo Alonso, Carlson Gracie Team
  • Green Belt, Judo
  • 3rd Degree Tae Kwon Do Black Belt

What patients can expect

A
One-on-one attention
Visits are centered on direct time with your physical therapist, not a handoff to a busy clinic flow.
B
Clear communication
Patients should leave visits understanding what is happening, why it matters, and what the next step is.
C
Plans that fit real life
Treatment should support your schedule, goals, and current capacity instead of forcing a generic template.
