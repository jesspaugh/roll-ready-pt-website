Before you book

  • Start with a discovery call if you want to ask questions before committing to an evaluation.
  • Choose an evaluation if you are ready for a full assessment and treatment plan.
  • Use follow-up visits after your initial evaluation to continue care and progress your goals.
  • Most visits happen at the gym — before or after class, between rounds, or at open mat — or curbside in the pop-up van in the gym lot.
